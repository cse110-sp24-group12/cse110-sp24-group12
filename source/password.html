To create a password:
1. Click on "Create Password"
2. Enter your password in the empty field, making sure that is is 6 or more characters
3. Click create and then enter a pin to help you recover your password if you lost it
4. After entering your pin, click create!

To enter our Dev Journal:
1. Enter your password into the empty field (refer to create password if you have not created one yet)
2. If you want us to automatically log you in, select "Remember Me" (If not, skip this step)
3. Click Unlock!
